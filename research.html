ACL 2024 Peer-Reviewed Publication

BlockNet: Multilingual Block Diagram Analysis

Unveiling the Power of Integration: Block Diagram Summarization through Local-Global Fusion

Research Problem

Block diagrams are fundamental in technical documentation but are difficult to digitize and summarize automatically, especially for multilingual content. Existing methods fail to capture both local details and global structure effectively.

Innovation

Introduced BlockNet, a novel fusion framework that integrates local OCR-based information extraction with global transformer-based understanding for comprehensive block diagram analysis in both English and Korean.

Methodology

  • Developed BD-EnKo: High-quality multilingual dataset for block diagram understanding
  • Created BlockSplit: OCR-based algorithm for precise local information extraction
  • Designed OCR-free transformer architecture for global structural understanding
  • Implemented local-global fusion mechanism for comprehensive analysis
