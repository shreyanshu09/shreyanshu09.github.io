2024

BlockNet: Multilingual Block Diagram Summarization

Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics · ACL 2024

Shreyanshu Bhushan et al.

A novel framework for understanding and summarizing block diagrams with multilingual output support. BlockNet addresses the challenge of extracting structured information from technical diagram images by combining computer vision with language generation, enabling automated summarization of complex flow charts and process diagrams commonly found in technical and scientific documents.

2022

Image-Based Document Summarization

Asia-Pacific Chapter of the ACL · AACL-IJCNLP 2022

Shreyanshu Bhushan et al.

Research on extracting and summarizing textual content from document images, integrating OCR pipelines with abstractive summarization models. The work explores techniques for handling noisy OCR outputs in summarization workflows, contributing to the field of document image understanding.
