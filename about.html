I'm an AI Engineer & Researcher at NEOALI Co. Ltd. in Seoul, building production systems around Document AI, LLMs, RAG, OCR and Vision-Language Models.

I completed my Bachelor of Science (Engineering, Exchange) at Kyungpook National University, followed by a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence at ABR Lab, KNU — where I focused on bridging NLP and Computer Vision with deep learning, image-based summarization and block diagram understanding.

Since joining NEOALI Co. Ltd. in May 2023, I've led research and engineering on products like LayGen (1st Place, Intel AI PC Innovation Challenge 2024), AskMe, FormGen, VLMFusion OCR, SlideGen, HanForge, and a medical document disease extractor — each shipping to real enterprise deployments.

My research has been published at ACL 2024 and AACL-IJCNLP 2022, and I'm a co-inventor on a filed patent for an AI-Based Method and System for Understanding Document Structures.

I care most about on-device and offline-first AI: systems that treat user documents as private-by-default, run locally when they can, and don't compromise on format fidelity or answer quality.
